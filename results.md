Ailment Cappings
•
Normal delivery: Rs. 25,000
•
C-Section delivery: Rs. 25,000
•
Coverage: First two living children only
•
Waiting period: Covered from day one
•
Twin delivery: 100% of maternity limit (Rs. 25,000)
•
Life-threatening complications: Covered up to Family SI limit
•
Pregnancy hospitalization without delivery: Rs. 5,00,000
•
Medical termination of pregnancy: Not covered
•
Mother expenses: Not covered
